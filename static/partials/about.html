This web site allows the comparison of any Open Access (OA) policy registered in ROARMAP database with the Horizon2020 funding program OA requirements.

Data are fetched directly from the ROARMAP web site and web API. The sources for the Horizon OA requirements are the following:

  1. Guidelines on Open Access to Scientific Publications and Research Data in Horizon 2020 (Version 2.1, 15 February 2016): § 3. Mandate on open access to publications
  2. H2020 General Model Grant Agreement — Multi (Version 2.1, 1 October 2015): ARTICLE 29 — DISSEMINATION OF RESULTS — OPEN ACCESS — VISIBILITY OF EU FUNDING

The criteria used to determine the level of compliance of a ROARMAP-classified policy with H2020 OA requirements have been documented in this document.
